FAQs

1. What is the other name of Virat Kohli?

King Kohli

2. What are some of Virat Kohli's notable cricket records and achievements?

Virat Kohli has numerous records to his name, including being one of the fastest batsmen to reach various milestones in international cricket. He holds records for the fastest to reach 8,000, 9,000, 10,000, and 11,000 runs in One Day Internationals (ODIs). Kohli is also known for his consistent performance in Test cricket and has achieved several accolades in T20 cricket.

3. Has Virat Kohli received any awards or honors for his cricketing achievements?

Yes, Virat Kohli has received numerous awards for his outstanding performances in cricket. He has been honored with the Rajiv Gandhi Khel Ratna, India's highest sporting honor, and has received the Sir Garfield Sobers Trophy for the ICC Cricketer of the Year multiple times. Kohli has also been named as one of the Wisden Cricketers of the Year, among other prestigious awards.
